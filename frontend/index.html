🌾 About KrishiSetu

India's Smartest Millet Value Chain Platform

Millets (Shree Anna) are nutritionally rich, climate-resilient crops vital to sustainable agriculture. KrishiSetu digitalizes the entire millet ecosystem — removing middlemen, ensuring fair prices, and empowering smallholder farmers.

🏪
Unified Digital Marketplace Connects farmers, FPOs, SHGs, processors, and consumers on one transparent platform.
💰
Fair Pricing Engine Real-time market data and pricing ensures farmers receive the true value of their produce.
🔍
Farm-to-Fork Traceability Consumers can trace the complete supply chain — from cultivation to product delivery.
📶
Accessible Anywhere Designed for low-bandwidth rural connectivity with multilingual mobile support.
⚡ Platform Features

Everything the Millet Ecosystem Needs

Six powerful capabilities to transform the way millets are grown, traded, and consumed across India.

🏪

Direct Marketplace

Farmers and SHGs list their millet produce directly. Buyers browse, compare, and order — zero middlemen, maximum margin for farmers.

📊

Real-Time Pricing Engine

Live market price updates help farmers make informed selling decisions and ensure buyers get competitive, transparent rates at all times.

🔗

Farm-to-Fork Traceability

Every product is digitally tagged with origin, farmer details, certifications, and supply chain data — complete consumer transparency.

🌍

Multilingual & Offline Access

Available in multiple Indian languages. Works seamlessly in low-connectivity rural areas through progressive web app technology.

💳

Integrated Payment Gateway

Secure digital payments directly to farmer bank accounts. Supports UPI, net banking, and multiple payment modes for seamless transactions.

🏆

Quality Certification Hub

SHGs, FPOs, and farmers can showcase certifications. Verified quality badges build consumer trust and command premium pricing.

🔄 How It Works

Simple Steps, Powerful Impact

The KrishiSetu workflow is designed to be intuitive for first-time smartphone users to enterprise buyers.

1

Register & Verify

Farmers, FPOs, SHGs, processors, and consumers create accounts via the mobile app or web dashboard. The system verifies credentials through the authentication service.

👤 Authentication Module
2

List Your Products

Farmers and SHGs upload millet product details — type, quantity, price, location, and certifications. Listings become visible instantly in the marketplace.

📦 Product Listing Service
3

Browse the Marketplace

Consumers, processors, and buyers discover millet products with origin details, farmer information, pricing, and available quantity — fully transparent.

🏪 Marketplace Engine
4

Place an Order

Buyers select suitable products and place orders through the platform. Order details are stored in the database and the seller is instantly notified.

🛒 Order Management
5

Secure Payment

Buyers complete payment through integrated gateways. The system confirms transactions and triggers notifications to both buyer and seller instantly.

💳 Payment Gateway
6

Order Prepared & Dispatched

After payment confirmation, the farmer or SHG prepares the order. The product is delivered and order status is updated live on the platform.

🚚 Logistics Tracking
7

Traceability & Review

Consumers access the full supply chain — farmer details, cultivation location, certifications, and processing data — building complete trust in the product.

🔍 Traceability System
8

Admin Monitoring

The admin dashboard enables real-time monitoring of users, listings, transactions, and platform health — ensuring smooth and secure operations.

⚙️ Admin Dashboard
